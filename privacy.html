Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Data We Collect

YuiDrop ("the Service") collects only the minimum data required to function: TikTok OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens) for API authentication. These tokens are stored locally and are never shared with third parties.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Service does not collect, store, or process: personal information, email addresses, location data, browsing history, device information, or any user-generated content beyond what is necessary to perform the posting operation.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service communicates with TikTok's official API (TikTok for Developers) solely for the purpose of posting videos. TikTok's own privacy policy applies to data processed on their servers.

4. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored until revoked. You can revoke access at any time via your TikTok account settings.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the account owner via the associated social media channels.
